# Identification Bridge

Fix a determining class `C_det` of endpoint observables.

The identification bridge requires:

- continuity of the determining observables on the admissible class,
- exact coherence target `eps_coh = 0`,
- uniqueness of the endpoint representative inside the determining class.

Under these conditions, the canonical endpoint in `paper/YAU_TIAN_DONALDSON_PREPRINT.md` is identified with the intended target class.
